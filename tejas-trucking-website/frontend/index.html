Tejas Trucking & Construction L.L.C. is a Houston-based, Texas-licensed LLC committed to delivering dependable, high-quality services to clients across the greater Houston metropolitan area.

Located in the Northside community at 719 W 27th Street, we bring local knowledge, professional equipment, and a hardworking crew to every job — no matter the size or complexity.
